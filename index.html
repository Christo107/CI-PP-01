About Us

Fresh

All of our ingredients are sourced from local suppliers ensuring the freshest taste and highest quality.

We believe in supporting producers in the local community to create a sustainable food production model and build positive working relationships with our suppliers.

We ensure only the best quality ingredients go into making our food items.

Organic

We buy produce only from suppliers who follow strict procedures in their food production. This includes not using chemical pesticides or fertilisers.

We believe that organically grown food tastes far better, and is sustainable into the future.

Come in and try our food for yourself and we think you'll agree.

Vegetarian

All our food and beverages are 100% vegetarian and certified by the Vegetarian Society of Ireland.

We have a selection of vegan food items also available, along with items that are gluten free.

Please ask our staff about any dietary requiements. We are happy to answer any of your questions.
